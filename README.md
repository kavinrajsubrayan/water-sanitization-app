# H2O Harmony - Clean Water & Sanitation Mobile Application

## Title / Role
**Team Leader – H2O Harmony (Clean Water & Sanitation Mobile Application)**

## Problem
Access to clean water and awareness of water scarcity remain pressing issues in many communities. There is a lack of real-time updates, educational resources, and community engagement platforms focused on sustainable water management.

## Tools Used
- **Flutter** – For cross-platform mobile app development
- **Firebase** – For backend services and real-time data management
- **Visual Studio Code** – As the main development environment

## What I Did
As the **Team Leader** under the group name **Fraud Guard**, I led the planning, development, and delivery of the **H2O Harmony** mobile app. I was responsible for overseeing the full development cycle, including:
- UI/UX design decisions
- Task delegation
- Testing
- Final deployment

### Major Features Implemented:
- **Real-time Water Scarcity Alerts**: Provides users with up-to-date alerts on water scarcity in their region.
- **Interactive Community Impact Dashboard**: Visualizes the progress of local initiatives and water-related projects.
- **Educational Content Integration**: Offers users access to water conservation tips, facts, and resources.
  
### Ensured project alignment with:
- **UN Sustainable Development Goal 6**: Clean Water & Sanitation.

## Challenges + Learning
- **Challenge**: Coordinating team members with varying skill levels on a tight timeline.
  - **Solution & Learning**: Applied agile planning and clear communication to keep tasks on track. Learned how to lead a multidisciplinary team effectively and resolve technical blockers quickly.

- **Challenge**: Integrating **Firebase** for real-time alerts and data updates.
  - **Learning**: Gained hands-on experience in backend integration and learned how to manage cloud-based data flow efficiently.

## Outcome
Successfully completed and submitted the **H2O Harmony app** in **APK format** for a competition. The app provides users with:
- Essential water-related alerts

The project strengthened my leadership, **Flutter development**, and **Firebase integration** skills.
